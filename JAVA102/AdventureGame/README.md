## Locations
### Safe House
* Feature: Life regeneration
### Cave
* Obstacle: Zombie(1-3)
* Feature: War + treasure
* Item: Food
### Forest
* Obstacle: Vampire(1-3)
* Feature: War + treasure
* Item: Firewood
### River
* Obstacle: Bear(1-3)
* Feature: War + treasure
* Item: Water
### Quarry
* Obstacle: Snake(1-5)
* Feature: War and treasure
* Item: Money, weapon or armor
### Toolstore
* Feature: Buying items
* Weapons: Gun, rifle, and sword
* Armor: Light, medium, and heavy
